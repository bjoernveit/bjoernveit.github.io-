Bodyweight-Training

Why do I think beeing an athlete is a prefect combination to beeing a Computersicentist, you might ask.
Well, going to your limits and beyond, will clear you head completely, which is most usefull, when your stuck with a problem of any kind. If you don't belive me just try it yourself, next time your stuck finding an error in your code and can't seem to wrap your head around it. Do an exhausting 30 min workout. You can find plenty you can do anywhere anytime on the internet (you can ask me, too). Afterwards you get a fresh start at takling the problem at hand. Obviously I can't garantee your success afterwards, but I'm sure you have a better chance of solving the problem this way, worst case you get more fit. I know what you're thinking "I can't just do a workout in the office" and I guess you are right, just do something when you get home then. Your head will just be more clear in general and it brings great balance to the office/university life.
I don't want to tell you how to live your life, but I can't imagine my life without sports. I'm just saying pushing your limits and having exhausted your whole body completly is a great feeling for me, but you schould check it out for yourself. Since bodyweight workouts don't require a membership to anything you don't have any excuses anyway.

Local Hack Day - University Bremen

The Local Hack Day, is a national MLH event, where all over the world small local 12h Hackdays took place at the same time. One of which I organized with 3 fellow students. At hackdays like these you meet new people, learn new stuff and simply use the great work athmosphere to work on your own projects. Last year one of our sponsors (UBiMAX), brought to two Microsof Hololenses to the event.
If I sparked your intresst, I'm sorry the event took place last december. I know its still a long way to go, but we are defenetly looking to orginize an other one at the end of this year.
